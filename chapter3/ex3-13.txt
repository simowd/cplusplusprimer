Exercise 3.13: How many elements are there in each of the following vectors? What
are the values of the elements?
(a)vector<int> v1;
(b)vector<int> v2(10);
(c)vector<int> v3(10, 42);
(d)vector<int> v4{10};
(e)vector<int> v5{10, 42};
(f)vector<string> v6{10};
(g)vector<string> v7{10, "hi"};

a. Empty.
b. 10 elements initialized as zero.
c. 10 elements initalized as 42.
d. 1 element. 10.
e. 2 elements. 10 and 42.
f. 10 empty string elements.
g. 10 string elements initialized as "hi".
